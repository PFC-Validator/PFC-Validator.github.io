PFC

PFC has always been about doing what is right for the average person, and doing what’s right for the chain. It’s why we got into running validators in the first place, and why we can be very vocal in our positions.

We have been operating validating services for over 18 months in the COSMOS ecosystem, and run relayers and provide services like STEAK liquid steaking for people. We currently operate around 30 different validators for ourselves and others on various Cosmos chains. Our team includes industry veterans who have run large scale internet operations including brand names like AOL & Microsoft.

To provide better resilience & decentralisation, our nodes operate across 3 different hosting providers in multiple locations.

Our team has been developing & running software for decades. We're good at it.

We're also a big fan of the community, and we're always looking for new projects to join us on, and are honored to be recognized by Stader Labs as one of their Community Validators.

Join our Discord

PFC also offers hosting solutions for other people who would prefer to focus on other aspects of their business, and is always building tools & things that help people make validating easier.
